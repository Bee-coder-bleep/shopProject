About Our Company

In 1995 at a small town of Kwa Haraka in Nyandarua County, Kanini Haraka Enterprises was born. Quality products and services, innovation and trust are the values that Kanini Haraka Enterprises limited was founded on and has endeavored to maintain since then.

Kanini Haraka Enterprises ltd has been guided by our vision, mission, goals and our value philosophy to scale through the ever changing economic and business terrain.

Under our guiding principles, we have been able to establish a strong vibrant distributorship of fast moving consumer goods at locations most convenient for our customers and by keeping our operating hours flexible and convenient. This is designed in a manner that will not compromise our promise of guaranteed quality of products and services.
